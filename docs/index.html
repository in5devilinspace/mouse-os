How it works

Three moving parts. Zero cloud.

01 · EARS

Voice

Your microphone feeds an offline recognizer constrained to the Mouse OS grammar. "point to firefox" comes out as text, instantly.

02 · BRAIN

Intent

A parser turns words into one precise instruction — Point(target="firefox") — and asks aloud when it's ambiguous.

03 · HAND

Pointer

A kernel-level virtual mouse glides the real cursor there and clicks — then the voice confirms: "clicked."
